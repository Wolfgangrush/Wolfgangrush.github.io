## 6 · Vidhi Likhit — the litigator's toolbench 🔒
The reasoning layer for Indian litigation — deterministic legal engines, not black-box generation:

- **Vidhi Likhit** (solo + enterprise) — AI-native Indian law school + agentic research over 10,000+ bare acts.
- **NYAYA-CHECK** — encodes a cause of action as defeasible logic and type-checks your fact-sheet.
- **PRAMAAN** — s.63 BSA electronic-evidence engine: hash-at-capture, chain-of-custody, the admissibility certificate.
- **PRATIPAKSHA** — opposing-counsel red-team for any Indian legal draft.
- **VIDHI-DELTA · ASTHIR** — anti-hallucination citation-verifier · precedent-instability radar.

*Private — access on request.*
